Reroom: GPT-Powered Room Material Swapper

Reroom is a Django-based GPT wrapper that lets users upload photos of their rooms and virtually swap wall paints and floor tiles. Users can choose from predefined material samples or upload a reference room or tile, and the system applies the transformation to their room image.

Key Features:

  • Image Uploads: Users upload room photos in common formats (JPG/PNG).
  • Material Library: Predefined wall and floor samples for quick try-ons.
  • Reference-Based Swap: Upload a reference room/tile to match style and color.
  • Guided GPT Flow: GPT assists with material selection and transformation prompts.
  • Export: Download transformed images for sharing or saving.
