Real-Time Insurance Call Simulator

A real-time conversation simulator where an AI bot acts as the customer and human agents follow a guided script. The system uses the latest GPT real-time capabilities to emulate insurance inquiry calls, capture sentiment and compliance, and study how agents perform when they know an AI is monitoring the interaction.

Study Goals:

  • Behavioral Insights: Observe how agent behavior changes with AI monitoring.
  • Script Adherence: Measure compliance to scripted flows and disclosures.
  • Quality Metrics: Track empathy, clarity, and resolution effectiveness.

Core Features:

  • Realtime Dialog: WebRTC/GPT real-time voice for natural, low-latency calls.
  • Agent App: Agents initiate sessions and follow study scripts with prompts.
  • Scenario Control: Vary customer intents, objections, and policy constraints.
  • Analytics: Conversation transcripts, scoring, and insight dashboards.
