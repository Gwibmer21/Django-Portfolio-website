Django Survey and Report (with AI)

django-survey-and-report is a comprehensive Django application for creating, managing, and analyzing surveys with advanced reporting capabilities.

Key Features

  • Survey Creation: Build surveys with multiple question types, categories, and validation rules.
  • Multi-language: 12+ languages (EN, FR, DE, ES, ZH, JA, ...).
  • Advanced Reporting: Export CSV or generate professional PDF via LaTeX.
  • Visual Analytics: Pie, polar, Sankey, and custom charts.
  • AI Integration: GPT-powered survey generation capabilities.
  • Survey Builder: Drag-and-drop interface.
  • Response Management: Track and analyze responses with detailed reporting.
